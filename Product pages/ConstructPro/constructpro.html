The Problem

Digitize Your Construction Operations

Construction companies face critical challenges with manual processes that cause budget overruns, material loss, and delayed decisions.

Poor Material Tracking

No visibility into material usage per site. Wastage and theft go undetected until budgets explode.

Hidden Site Expenses

Expenses scattered across registers and receipts. No centralized visibility for management oversight.

Manual Attendance & Wages

Paper-based attendance invites fake entries. Wage calculations are error-prone and time-consuming.

No Daily Work Records

Daily progress goes undocumented. Management relies on verbal updates and physical site visits.

Multi-Site Chaos

Managing multiple sites from one office is impossible without a centralized digital platform.

Limited Reporting

End-of-project realization of budget overruns. No real-time analytics for corrective action.

How It Works

From Site to Office — All Data in One Place

A streamlined workflow connecting site engineers, supervisors, and management in real-time.

1

Create Sites

Admin creates project sites with budgets, locations, and assigns site engineers or supervisors.

2

Record Daily Work

Site users log daily activities with photos and descriptions. Full historical progress trail maintained.

3

Track Materials

Add materials used on site with quantity, cost, and photos. Submit for admin approval before recording.

4

Manage Labour

Register workers, mark daily attendance, and track hours worked for accurate wage calculations.

5

Upload Bills

Record vendor bills with GST details and photos. Build a complete digital expense audit trail.

6

Monitor & Report

Admin dashboard shows all sites, budgets, expenses, and progress. Generate reports for stakeholders.

User Roles

Centralized Control, Decentralized Operations

Two distinct roles, each with purpose-built tools for their workflow.

Admin Dashboard

Full control over all sites, users, budgets, and reports from the head office.

  • View and manage all project sites
  • Approve or reject material requests
  • Monitor daily work logs across sites
  • Track labour strength and attendance
  • Analyze site-wise costs and budgets
  • Generate comprehensive reports

Site User Dashboard

Streamlined daily operations entry for site engineers and supervisors.

  • Add materials with photos and quantities
  • Record daily work activities with site photos
  • Mark labour attendance and hours worked
  • Upload vendor bills and expense records
  • View site budget and remaining balance
  • Access work history and past entries
