Circulation Logic

How the Reservation Workflow Operates

An automated, fair-access workflow to manage book scarcity transparently.

1

Discovery

Student identifies a book via Advanced Search across catalogs, thesis, journals, and question banks.

2

Reserve or Queue

If available, reserve and collect. If checked out, join the digital queue automatically.

3

Auto-Notify & Pickup

Next student gets a 24-hour Priority Pickup window. Missed? Moves to the next person.

4

30-Day Fair Access

Unfulfilled reservations auto-close after 30 days. Books stay in motion, never trapped.

5

Return & Check-in

Streamlined return with automatic fine calculation. Holiday-aware ledger ensures fairness.

6

History & Insights

Complete chronological log. Every book, every student, every fine — tracked and reportable.
