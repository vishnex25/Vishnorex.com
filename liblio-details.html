Circulation Logic

How the Reservation Workflow Operates

Liblio employs an automated, fair-access workflow to manage book scarcity transparently.

1

Discovery

Student identifies a book via Advanced Search across catalogs, thesis, journals, and question banks.

2

Reserve or Queue

If available, reserve and collect within a specified window. If checked out, join the digital queue automatically.

3

Auto-Notify & Pickup

When returned, the next student receives a 24-hour Priority Pickup window. Missed? Automatically moves to the next.

4

30-Day Fair Access

Reservations unfulfilled within 30 days are auto-closed. Books stay in motion — never trapped in dead queues.

5

Return & Check-in

Streamlined return process with automatic fine calculation. Holiday-aware ledger ensures fair penalties.

6

History & Insights

Complete chronological log of all transactions. Every book, every student, every fine — tracked and reportable.
